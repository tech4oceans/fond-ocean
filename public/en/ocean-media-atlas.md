# Ocean Media Atlas

![ocean-media-atlas preview](../../assets/public-previews/en/ocean-media-atlas.svg)

This page maps a first high-signal set of media outlets, editors, and public communication models that shape how ocean stories are written, framed, and circulated.

Verified against official public pages on 13 May 2026.

## Focus

- Oceanographic Magazine: visual-first ocean magazine with strong columnist and expedition energy.
- Hakai Magazine: archival long-form model for coasts, science, society, and narrative journalism.
- Mongabay Oceans: fast, source-rich, accountability-oriented environmental reporting.
- Waterfront Alliance / City of Water Day: civic water communication and public urban participation.

## Why It Matters

Ocean Fund needs more than publication volume. It needs to understand the editorial architectures, public voices, and communication habits that make ocean writing legible and memorable.

## Working Rule

Learn from structure, tone, cadence, and imagery, but do not imitate another outlet's identity.
